Our Projects

Current Project: Offshore Wind Farm Design

Our current project focuses on designing an offshore wind farm for the Department of Energy's annual Collegiate Wind Competition. This project involves:

  • Designing a stable and efficient offshore wind turbine
  • Developing a comprehensive siting plan for the wind farm
  • Creating a detailed financial model for the project
  • Addressing environmental and regulatory challenges

The team is working on maximizing structural stability and performance efficiency in harsher, offshore conditions to ensure continuous power output. We're also analyzing government and maritime regulations, as well as tax and other financial risks to determine the best lease areas, turbines to use, and bid price.
